# Manuscript Blueprint

`manuscript_blueprint.json` is the paper argument before LaTeX rendering.

## Required Fields

- `title`
- `central_argument`
- `section_plan`
- `claim_to_experiment_map`

## Section Plan

Include at least:

- Abstract
- Introduction
- Related Work and Design Rationale
- Problem Statement
- Method
- Planned Study Design
- Planned Results Presentation
- Risks
- Discussion
- Conclusion

## Claim Map

Every manuscript claim should map to:

- experiment or planned evidence
- evidence status
- target section

Claims without planned evidence stay out of finished prose.
