# Formula Requirements

The Method section must be mathematical before it is written as prose.

## Required Formula Types

- Data or task definition.
- At least one formula for each method module.
- At least one loss term for each method module.
- One total objective, preferably `\mathcal{L}_{total}`.

## Symbol Hygiene

For every important symbol, define:

- What it represents.
- Shape or set when useful.
- Whether it is input, prediction, latent variable, parameter, or target.

## Forbidden

- Pseudo-formulas that only restate prose.
- Undefined losses.
- Measured numerical results before experiments.
- Claims that the formula proves performance.
