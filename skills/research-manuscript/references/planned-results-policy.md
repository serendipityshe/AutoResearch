# Planned Results Policy

No experiment result exists until an experiment report, CSV, log, or approved evidence file exists.

## Before Experiments

- Use `TBD`, `to be filled`, or empty planned cells.
- Do not write measured Dice, IoU, HD95, AUC, accuracy, p-values, confidence intervals, or percentages.
- Do not imply that a hypothesis has been validated.

## Allowed

- Planned table shells.
- Planned metrics.
- Planned statistical tests.
- Expected or hypothesis language outside the Results table.

## Forbidden

- Numeric result values in planned result tables.
- Superlatives such as `outperforms`, `achieves`, or `significantly improves` unless supported by experiment evidence.
