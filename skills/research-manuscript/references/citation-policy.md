# Citation Policy

Bibliography is evidence-filtered.

## Stable Locators

A source may enter bibliography only if it has at least one:

- `url`
- `doi`
- `pmid`
- `pmcid`
- `arxiv_id`
- `citation_key`
- `local_path`
- `paper_or_repo_url`

## Citation Plan

Write `citation_plan.json` with:

- included sources
- excluded sources
- exclusion reason

Use citations for background, datasets, baselines, and method context. Do not use citations as evidence for unrun experimental results.
